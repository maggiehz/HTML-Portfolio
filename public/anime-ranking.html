The Best Animes According to Maggie

My top 3 animes of all time.

Attack on Titan

This is my favorite anime of all time. It has great characters, a deep and well-thought plot with mysteries to be solved, and a parallel to the world we live in and how humans interact with eachother.
It also has meaningful relationships between characters and depicts the dynamic between two different sides of the same war and how the people view eachother. A great watch for people interested in action and mystery.

Haikyu!!

This is my second favorite anime of all time. It is an inspirational depiction of driven athletes with a passion of volleyball. It shows perseverance, passion, and the emotions of having a competitive spirit in something you love.
Great for people who want to have a laugh, a cry, and become inspired.

Fruit's Basket

This is my third favorite anime of all time. It has interesting characters that represet each of the zodiac animals and a main chracter who has had a difficult life but continues to hold a positive outlook in life and respectful, caring, and kind to those around her.
It also has a lot of drama and suspense but is a sweet story to unravel. Great for people interested in romance, friendship, and drama.
